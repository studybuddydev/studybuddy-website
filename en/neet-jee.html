Common Questions

Is this app suitable for NEET 2026 preparation?

Absolutely. StudyBuddy is designed to handle the rigorous schedules of NEET aspirants. You can track long study hours, separate Biology, Physics, and Chemistry time logs, and view your consistency over months.

How does the Pomodoro timer help with JEE Mains?

The Pomodoro technique (and our flexible variations) prevents burnout. By breaking your 12+ hour study sessions into focused chunks with short breaks, you maintain high retention rates for complex JEE topics.

Can I track Physics, Chemistry, and Maths separately?

Yes! You can tag your study sessions by subject. This allows you to see if you are neglecting any subject and ensure a balanced preparation strategy.

Is the app free for students?

StudyBuddy offers a robust free version that includes the timer, basic stats, and todo lists. We also have a Plus version for advanced analytics, but the core features for NEET/JEE prep are accessible to everyone.
