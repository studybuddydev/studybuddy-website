## Key Actions

### Open the Web App
Navigate to https://app.studybuddy.it to access the full StudyBuddy experience in the browser.

### Get Download Links
- iOS (App Store): https://apps.apple.com/app/id6670455793
- Android (Google Play): search "StudyBuddy" by studybuddydev

### Navigate to a Section
Append the anchor to the homepage URL, e.g. `https://studybuddy.it/en/#features` or `https://studybuddy.it/en/#contact`.
